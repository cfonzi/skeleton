Hours of Operation

We open daily at 12:00 and close about 1 hour after sunset. For Memorial Day weekend, we will open at 10:00 each day.

Please note our weather policy is always to close with a lasting steady rain or when the temperature or heat index exceeds 100 degrees. If the weather is ever ugly, please call the shop (703.830.7898) or visit Facebook (Peterson's Ice Cream Depot) for information about our hours that day.
